How to convert km to miles

To convert kilometres to miles use a conversion rate of 0.621. This means for every 1 km in distance it is 0.621 miles.

Worked Example 30km to Miles:

30 (km) * 0.621 = 18.63 (miles)

How this tool helps you Travel Better

In the UK, we measure and report distances in miles. For example, a motorway sign might say “London 110” — meaning it’s 110 miles to London.

Because we’re used to thinking in miles, we naturally develop a sense of how far that feels, how long it will take to drive, and when we might need to stop. In this case, you might know from experience that 110 miles takes about two hours, so you decide to drive straight through or take a break after an hour.

But when you're travelling in Europe or other countries that use kilometres, that instinctive understanding disappears. Road signs and maps might show distances in kilometres, making it harder to judge how long a journey will take or when to take a break. For example, seeing “180 km to Paris” might leave you unsure if that’s a quick drive or a long haul.

That’s where this simple conversion tool helps. Just enter the distance in kilometres, and it will convert it to miles instantly — so you can make quick, confident decisions while planning or on the road. It’s a small thing, but it can take away some of the stress of navigating unfamiliar places.
