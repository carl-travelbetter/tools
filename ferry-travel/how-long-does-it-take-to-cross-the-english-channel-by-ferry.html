Why You Can Trust Our Cross-Channel Information

This tool was built from over a decade of first-hand travel. As a family (Dad, Mum and three kids), we have crossed the English Channel 14 times over the last 15 years, using multiple ferry operators and the Eurotunnel.

We have driven through Normandy, commuted south to Bergerac, driven to Disneyland Paris, and navigated long-haul road trips across Europe all the way to Tuscany, Italy. We know the stress of booking the wrong crossing with a car full of luggage.

To build this tool, we combined our real-world road-tripping insights with structured, up-to-date data on crossing times, real-time availability, hidden costs, route options, and port accessibility.

We hope this tool helps you find the perfect route. Have a fantastic adventure! — [Carl - aka Travelbetter Dad]
