Timers and Countdowns

Managing time is an essential skill for effective travel but can be a real challenge for Neurodivergent families.

For example, a number of Neurdivergent people can be time blind and really struggle with the very basics of keeping on schedule.

To help with these challenges we have created a number of simple to use countdown and timer apps designed to take the stress out of time management and help you travel better.

Holiday Countdown

An application to help you create one or more holiday countdowns for your upcoming trips to keep track of deadlines and share with others to help them as well.

About Travelbetter

Travelbetter is a family travel project created to support quiet, anxious and neurodiverse travellers. We believe travel should feel calm, kind and accessible — not overwhelming. Through simple tools, thoughtful guides and lived experience, we help families plan trips that truly work for them.

Our mission is to help familes Explore More and Worry Less
