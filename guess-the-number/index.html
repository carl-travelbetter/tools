How to Play Guess The Number

  1. Enter your guess
    This should be a number in the range shown.
  2. Get your result
    Correct, or incorrect with a hint to go higher or lower.
  3. Keep Guessing
    Keep guessing until your tries run out or you guess the number.
  4. Level Up or Down
    Finding it too easy or hard, then simply change the levels and increase or decrease the number range.
  5. Re-start
    Restart your game any time you want.
  6. Challenge a friend
    Find out if your friends are as good as you on this game.
